# Sentiment_Analysis
Basic about Sentiment Analysis with Example.


## How does it work?

Step 1. Tokenization
-Example :- The Movie was Great! | > 
     . The
     . movie
     . was
     . great
     . !
     
Step 2. Data Cleaning | >  . ! 

Step 3. Removing Stop Words | >  . The  . was

Step 4. Classification | > 
Positive : +1
Nagative : -1
Neutral : 0

* Classify them as positive negative and neutral.
